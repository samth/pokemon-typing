## How to Play

1. **Choose Your Mode**: Toggle between Pokemon and My Little Pony
2. **Enable Advanced Mode** (optional): Check the box for full sentences instead of simple ones
3. **Start Typing**: Click on the speech bubble or just start typing
4. **Watch Your Progress**: Characters turn green (correct) or red (incorrect) as you type
5. **Complete Sentences**: Finish to see your accuracy and grade
6. **Keep Practicing**: Build your streak and improve!

## Keyboard Layout

The app focuses on home row keys plus nearby letters for beginners:

```
    e  r  t
  a  s  d  f  g  h  j  k  l
    c  v  n  m
```

**Home row** (green): a, s, d, f, j, k, l
**Used keys** (blue): e, r, t, c, v, n, m
